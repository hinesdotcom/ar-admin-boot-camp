# Side Artist Agreements

## Overview

Side artist agreements are contracts with artists who contribute performances to a recording but are not the main artist.

## Purpose

- Define payment obligations
- Clarify credit and publishing
- Manage rights and royalties
- Protect all parties

## Agreement Components

### Artist Information
- Side artist name
- Contribution description
- Instrument/vocals performed
- Recording date

### Payment Terms
- Session fee
- Union scale requirements
- Residuals/royalties
- Expense reimbursement

### Rights & Publishing
- Master rights assignment
- Publishing ownership split
- Sync rights
- Sampling restrictions

### Delivery & Quality
- Recording requirements
- DQ standards
- Revision policy
- Timeline

### Credits
- Album credit line
- Metadata requirement
- Publishing registration
- PRO affiliation

## Union Considerations

### AFM (American Federation of Musicians)
- Scale payments required
- Session paperwork
- Union pension contributions
- Reuse payments

### SAG-AFTRA
- Commercial use implications
- Residual payments
- Approval rights
- Credit requirements

## Negotiation Points

- Session payment amount
- Royalty participation
- Publishing split
- Credit placement
- Reuse compensation

## Important Clauses

- Grant of rights
- Representations and warranties
- Indemnification
- Reversion provisions
- Payment conditions
