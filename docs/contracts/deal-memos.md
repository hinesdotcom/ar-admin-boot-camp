# Deal Memos

## Overview

Deal memos are preliminary agreements that outline the basic terms before formal contracts are executed.

## Deal Memo Purpose

- Quick negotiation of key terms
- Binding commitment on essentials
- Faster project initiation
- Bridge to full contracts

## Key Deal Memo Elements

### Basic Terms
- Parties involved
- Payment amount
- Delivery timeline
- Services described
- Budget code

### Rights & Ownership
- Master ownership
- Publishing rights
- Sync rights
- Mechanical rights

### Deliverables
- Formats required
- Quality standards
- Delivery method
- Revision limits

### Payment Terms
- Amount and schedule
- Recoupability
- Approval conditions
- Late payment penalties

## Deal Memo Process

### Negotiation
- Artist/vendor discussion
- Terms agreement
- Mutual understanding
- Key issue resolution

### Drafting
- Template selection
- Custom terms entry
- Legal review
- Formatting

### Execution
- Signature obtaining
- Counterpart execution
- Copies distribution
- File storage

## Conversion to Full Contract

- Timeline and milestones
- Additional terms negotiation
- Legal finalization
- Formal execution
