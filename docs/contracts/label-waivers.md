# Label Waivers

## Overview

Label waivers are agreements where artists or rights holders waive certain claims or requirements related to a recording.

## Common Types of Waivers

### Rights Clearance Waiver
- Artist waives certain rights
- Streamlines approval process
- Limits liability exposure
- Accelerates project timeline

### Credit & Liability Waiver
- Release from naming/credit claims
- Acceptance of credit as provided
- Release of liability for errors
- Acknowledgment of good faith efforts

### Sampling/Interpolation Waiver
- Permission for sample use
- Royalty structure agreement
- Mechanical and sync rights
- Usage restrictions

### Union Scale Waiver
- Agreement to reduced compensation
- Waiver of union scale requirements
- Special circumstances arrangement
- Documentation for union approval

## Waiver Process

### Identification of Need
- Legal determination of requirement
- Rights holder identification
- Contact and negotiation
- Terms discussion

### Drafting
- Specific language development
- Legal review
- Rights holder explanation
- Documentation

### Execution
- Independent legal representation (recommended)
- Signature collection
- Counterpart exchange
- File storage

## Important Considerations

- Voluntary nature required
- Fair market consideration
- Independent counsel recommended
- Clear documentation
- Specificity of waived rights

## Union Waiver Special Rules

- AFM approval requirement
- SAG-AFTRA notification
- Scale payment still required
- Documentation requirements
- Filing with union
