# Side Artists

## Overview

Side artist clearance ensures proper documentation of performances by artists other than the main artist(s) on a recording.

## Clearance Documentation

### Side Artist Agreement
- Performance description
- Recording date
- Payment amount
- Credit requirements
- Publishing ownership

### Information Collection
- Full legal name
- Contact information
- Union affiliation
- Tax ID/SSN
- Direct deposit information

### Credit Requirements
- Album liner notes credit
- Metadata credit line
- Publishing database registration
- Digital asset metadata

## Union Considerations

### AFM (American Federation of Musicians)
- Scale payment compliance
- Session paperwork filing
- Union pension contributions
- Reuse payment tracking

### SAG-AFTRA
- Commercial use documentation
- Residual payment liability
- Approval and consent
- Credit requirements

## Payment Processing

### Session Payment
- Verification of performance
- Union scale compliance
- Tax withholding
- Payment execution

### Ongoing Obligations
- Residual payments tracking
- Rebroadcast compensation
- Union reporting
- Royalty calculations

## Documentation Checklist

- [ ] Side artist agreement signed
- [ ] Union documentation filed
- [ ] Tax forms W-9/W-8 collected
- [ ] Payment processed
- [ ] Credit verified
- [ ] Publishing registered
- [ ] File archived

## Special Circumstances

- International artists
- Union jurisdictional issues
- Multiple performances
- Featured vs. background
- Contractor vs. direct hire
