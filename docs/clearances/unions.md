# Unions

## Overview

Union compliance ensures fair compensation and working conditions for musicians, singers, and other performers.

## Major Music Unions

### AFM (American Federation of Musicians)
- Musicians and instrumentalists
- Session scale rates
- Pension and health coverage
- Work rules and conditions
- Session documentation requirements

### SAG-AFTRA (Screen Actors Guild - American Federation of Television and Radio Artists)
- Singers and vocalists
- Commercial and broadcast work
- Residual payment obligations
- Union approval requirements
- Credential verification

### IATSE (International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees)
- Technical crew
- Live performance crew
- Tour personnel
- Equipment specialists

## Scale Payments

### AFM Scale Rates (Current)
- Three-hour session: Approximately $200-300
- Overtime rates (hourly)
- Additional services (overdubs, etc.)
- Union pension contribution (11.5%)

### SAG-AFTRA Rates
- Session singer rates vary by use type
- Jingle rates
- Commercial rates
- Reuse payment rates

## Compliance Requirements

### Before Recording
- Union notification
- Contractor approval
- Rate confirmation
- Session authorization

### During Recording
- Session paperwork completion
- Proper documentation
- Authorization verification
- Payment preparation

### After Recording
- Session report filing
- Payment processing
- Reuse payment tracking
- Benefits contribution

## Common Union Issues

- Unauthorized performer hiring
- Scale payment violations
- Improper session documentation
- Missing union benefits
- Reuse payment non-compliance
