# Samples

## Overview

Sample clearance is the process of obtaining permission and paying royalties for using portions of previously recorded material in new recordings.

## Clearance Process

### Identification
- Sample source identification
- Percentage of sample calculated
- Master owner determination
- Publishing owner determination

### Contact & Negotiation
- Master owner contact
- License negotiation
- Royalty rate agreement
- Usage restrictions

### Documentation
- License agreement execution
- Royalty tracking
- Payment scheduling
- Metadata documentation

### Verification
- Legal review
- Completeness check
- Term compliance
- File storage

## Types of Clearances

### Master Clearance
- Permission from master owner
- Recording rights granted
- Mechanical usage included
- Backend royalties specified

### Publishing Clearance
- Permission from publisher
- Mechanical license
- Performance rights assignment
- Publishing split documentation

## Royalty Structures

### Flat Fee
- One-time payment
- Fixed amount agreed
- No future royalties
- Simpler accounting

### Percentage of Net
- Percentage of revenues
- Recoupable or non-recoupable
- Revenue tracking required
- Quarterly or annual payments

### Points Structure
- Percentage of album sales
- Similar to artist royalties
- Sales tracking required
- Payment based on copies sold

## Documentation

- Sample identification
- Royalty amount and type
- Payment schedule
- Term duration
- Territory coverage
- Reversion conditions

## Common Pitfalls

- Unauthorized sample use
- Incomplete license documentation
- Ownership disputes
- Royalty calculation errors
- Failure to register with PRO
