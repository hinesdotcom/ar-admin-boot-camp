# Recording Process

## Overview

The recording process is the creative and technical core of music production, managed and tracked by A&R administration.

## Recording Phases

### Pre-Production
- Song selection and arrangement
- Artist and producer meetings
- Studio selection and booking
- Budget allocation
- Equipment and session musician coordination

### Studio Recording
- Session scheduling
- Daily setup and soundcheck
- Recording sessions
- Technical troubleshooting
- Quality monitoring
- Progress tracking

### Overdubs & Layering
- Vocal overdubs
- Instrumental layering
- Guitar/bass/drum replacement
- Additional parts
- Editing and cleanup

### Recording Documentation
- Session notes
- Takes tracking
- Personnel logging
- Equipment used
- Technical issues noted

## Key Responsibilities

### A&R Administration
- Budget tracking
- Session scheduling
- Vendor payment processing
- Union compliance
- Contract management
- Timeline monitoring

### Recording Team
- Session direction
- Technical execution
- Creative decisions
- Quality assurance
- Issue resolution

## Recording Timeline

- Week 1-2: Preparation
- Week 3-6: Main recording
- Week 7-8: Overdubs
- Week 9: Additional sessions
- Week 10: Contingency/wrap-up

## Best Practices

- Plan thoroughly before studio time
- Maintain accurate session logs
- Monitor budget daily
- Communicate changes immediately
- Document all decisions
- Archive all recordings

## Deliverables

- Raw recording files
- Session notes
- Personnel list
- Equipment manifest
- Budget reconciliation
