# Producers

## Overview

Producers are creative leaders who shape the sound and direction of recordings.

## Producer Roles

### Executive Producer
- Overall creative vision
- Artist development
- Project selection
- Budget oversight
- Final approval authority

### Recording Producer
- Day-to-day session leadership
- Artist direction
- Sound design
- Performance guidance
- Technical decisions

### Co-Producer
- Collaborative creative role
- Specific segment responsibility
- Specialized expertise
- Shared decision-making

## Producer Selection

### Evaluation Criteria
- Previous work quality
- Genre expertise
- Artist compatibility
- Budget alignment
- Availability
- References

### Negotiation Points
- Advance payment
- Per-track fees
- Production royalties
- Backend points
- Credit and publishing
- Approval rights

## Producer Agreements

- Compensation structure
- Rights ownership
- Delivery expectations
- Revision terms
- Dispute resolution
- Insurance requirements

## Producer Management

### Communication
- Weekly check-ins
- Status updates
- Issue escalation
- Creative feedback
- Timeline monitoring

### Tracking
- Session scheduling
- Budget consumption
- Milestone completion
- Deliverable tracking
- Payment processing

## Producer Responsibilities

- Creative direction
- Sound engineering oversight
- Artist mentoring
- Quality assurance
- Timeline management
- Documentation and notes
