# Advances

## Overview

Artist advances are payments made against future royalties and must be tracked and reconciled carefully.

## Types of Advances

### Recording Advances
- Upfront payment for recording
- Recoupable from master royalties
- Typically non-returnable

### Signing Bonus
- Welcome payment for new artists
- Non-recoupable
- Separate accounting treatment

### Promotional Advances
- Earmarked for marketing
- Requires documentation
- Approval before payment

## Advance Processing

### Approval Workflow
1. Artist negotiation
2. Contract finalization
3. Finance department setup
4. Payment authorization
5. Bank transfer execution
6. Receipt and documentation

### Documentation Requirements
- Signed artist agreement
- Finance department memo
- Bank transfer details
- Receipt confirmation
- Account reconciliation

## Tracking & Reconciliation

### Recording Tracking
- Advance amount
- Recoupable status
- Recoupment progress
- Royalty offset

### Reporting
- Advance ledger
- Recoupment status
- Cash flow forecasting
- Artist accounting statements

## Best Practices

- Always wait for signed contract
- Verify artist identity
- Confirm bank details
- Document approval chain
- Maintain audit trail
