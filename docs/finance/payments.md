# Payments

## Overview

Timely, accurate vendor payments are essential for maintaining strong relationships and ensuring project continuity.

## Payment Process

### Step 1: Invoice Receipt
- Physical or email receipt
- Vendor details verification
- Invoice number and date
- Amount and terms

### Step 2: Validation
- PO matching
- Quantity/quality verification
- Pricing accuracy check
- Account coding

### Step 3: Approval
- Department manager review
- Budget confirmation
- Compliance verification
- Finance sign-off

### Step 4: Processing
- System entry
- Payment scheduling
- Bank details verification
- Tax documentation

### Step 5: Execution
- ACH transfer or check
- Payment confirmation
- Receipt documentation
- Reconciliation

## Payment Methods

### ACH Transfer
- Preferred method
- Faster processing
- Lower fees
- Audit trail

### Check Payment
- Legacy vendors
- International payments
- Special circumstances

### Credit Card
- Supplies and services
- Automatic reconciliation
- Expense tracking

## Payment Terms

### Standard Terms
- Net 30 (payment due 30 days from invoice)
- Net 60 (payment due 60 days from invoice)
- On demand (immediate payment required)

### Discount Terms
- 2/10 Net 30 (2% discount if paid within 10 days)
- Early payment discounts
- Volume discounts

## Tracking & Reporting

- Payment ledger
- Cash flow forecasting
- Vendor aging report
- Monthly reconciliation
