# Purchase Orders

## Overview

Purchase Orders (POs) are the standard mechanism for authorizing and tracking vendor expenses.

## PO Requirements

Every purchase over $500 requires a formal Purchase Order.

### PO Components
- Vendor name and details
- Itemized services/goods
- Pricing and terms
- Project allocation
- Budget code
- Approval signature

## PO Process

### Step 1: Request
- Service quotation received
- Budget verification
- Department approval
- PO creation in system

### Step 2: Authorization
- Budget owner sign-off
- Finance department review
- Senior management approval
- Vendor notification

### Step 3: Execution
- Vendor begins work
- Track delivery milestones
- Monitor budget consumption
- Document changes

### Step 4: Reconciliation
- Invoice receipt
- PO comparison
- Variance analysis
- Payment processing

## PO Management

### Changes & Amendments
- Budget impact analysis
- Approval workflow
- Vendor communication
- System documentation

### Closure
- Final invoice reconciliation
- Variance reporting
- Payment completion
- Archive and audit
