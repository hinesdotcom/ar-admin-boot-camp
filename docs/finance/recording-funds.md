# Recording Funds

## Overview

Recording funds are the total allocated budget for all recording-related expenses on a project.

## Fund Allocation

### Budget Breakdown
- Studio time: 40%
- Engineering/Production: 35%
- Personnel (session musicians, etc.): 15%
- Contingency: 10%

## Tracking Recording Funds

### In ATLAS
- Create recording fund budget
- Allocate by studio/engineer
- Track weekly expenditure
- Monitor contingency usage

### Status Reporting
- Weekly spend reports
- Variance analysis
- Remaining fund balance
- Projected overages

## Fund Control

### Authorization Rules
- Spend cap per session
- Approval thresholds
- Budget owner oversight
- Weekly reconciliation

### Change Management
- Fund adjustments require approval
- Contingency usage tracking
- Contract amendments
- Documentation

## Recording Fund Best Practices

- Establish clear limits
- Get quotes before commitment
- Track daily expenses
- Communicate variances immediately
- Maintain contingency reserves
- Archive all receipts

## Fund Closure

- Final reconciliation
- Contingency analysis
- Overage documentation
- Lessons learned review
