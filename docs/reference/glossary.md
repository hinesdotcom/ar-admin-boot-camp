# Glossary

## A

**ASCAP** - American Society of Composers, Authors, and Publishers; a performing rights organization.

**Advance** - Payment made to artists or rights holders, typically recoupable from future royalties.

**AFM** - American Federation of Musicians; union representing musicians.

**ATMOS** - Dolby Atmos immersive audio format with 7.1.4 channel configuration.

## B

**Backend** - Royalties or payments earned after initial sale or advance recoupment.

**BMI** - Broadcast Music, Inc.; a performing rights organization.

**Blanket License** - License covering multiple compositions from a publisher.

## C

**Clearance** - Obtaining permission and potentially paying royalties for using copyrighted material.

**Co-Publishing** - Shared ownership and administration of composition rights.

**Composition** - Musical work (melody and lyrics) distinct from the recording.

## D

**DQ** - Digital Quality; technical standards for delivery.

**DDEX** - Digital Release Information Exchange; standardized format for music metadata.

## E

**Engineer** - Technical professional recording, mixing, or mastering music.

## F

**Feature** - Featured artist; prominent artist on a recording beyond the main artist.

**FLAC** - Free Lossless Audio Codec; lossless audio format.

## G

**Grant of Rights** - Contractual transfer of ownership or permission to use.

## H

**Harry Fox Agency** - Mechanical rights administrator collecting and paying mechanical royalties.

## I

**IATSE** - International Alliance of Theatrical Stage Employees; union for crew.

**ID3 Tags** - Metadata embedded in MP3 files.

**IPI** - Interested Party Information; identifier for music industry participants.

**ISRC** - International Standard Recording Code; unique identifier for recordings.

**ISWC** - International Standard Musical Work Code; unique identifier for compositions.

## L

**Lacquer** - Master used in vinyl pressing to create metal stampers.

**LUFS** - Loudness Units relative to Full Scale; loudness measurement standard.

## M

**Master** - Sound recording distinct from the composition.

**Master Use License** - Permission from master owner to use specific recording.

**Mechanical License** - Permission to reproduce a composition.

**Mechanical Royalty** - Payment for reproduction rights.

**MLC** - Mechanical Licensing Collective; non-profit mechanical rights administrator.

## N

**Net Receipts** - Revenue after direct costs deducted.

## O

**Overdub** - Additional recording layered over existing material.

## P

**Producer** - Person responsible for overall sound and direction of recording.

**PRO** - Performing Rights Organization (ASCAP, BMI, SESAC).

**Publishing** - Administration of composition rights and collection of royalties.

**Purchase Order (PO)** - Formal authorization document for vendor services.

## R

**Recoupable** - Amount that can be deducted from future payments.

**Reversion** - Return of rights to original owner.

**Royalty** - Payment for use of copyrighted material.

## S

**SAG-AFTRA** - Screen Actors Guild; union for actors and singers.

**Sample** - Portion of recording used in new composition.

**SESAC** - Society of European Stage Authors and Composers; performing rights organization.

**Session** - Recording, mixing, or mastering appointment.

**Split** - Division of rights or royalties among multiple parties.

**Sync** - Synchronization license for visual media use.

## T

**Talent Release** - Agreement granting rights to use performer's performance.

**Test Pressing** - Sample vinyl pressing for quality approval.

## U

**Union** - Labor organization representing workers' rights.

**Upfront** - Payment made before services rendered or royalties earned.

## V

**Vendor** - Service provider (studio, engineer, distributor, etc.).

**Vinyl** - Physical format using grooves in plastic.

## W

**WAV** - Waveform Audio File Format; uncompressed audio format.

**Waiver** - Contractual release of rights or claims.

**Withholding** - Percentage of payment retained for tax purposes.

## Z

**Zone** - Geographic territory.
